#7: Stand Feet Together on Foam, Eyes Open

Tester: Place Tempur® foam on the floor. Assist client stepping onto foam, guard client and assist client off the foam after testing. Allow 2 trials, if needed. Have client step off the foam between trials. Time and score best trial.

Client: Step on the foam. Stand with your feet touching together; hands on your hips. Look straight ahead. Stand as still as you can until I say stop.
