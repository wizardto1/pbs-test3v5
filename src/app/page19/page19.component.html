#17: Rapid Step Length Forward

Tester: Place 2 pieces of tape, 24 in (60 cm) apart on the floor as measured by the front edge of each tape (See diagram item 13). “Able” means client steps forward across the farthest tape AND returns in 1 step within 3 s. No assistive device, no assistance, demonstrate the task. Allow 2 trials, if needed. Score best trial. Client chooses which leg steps.

Client: Stand with feet hip width apart, step on the tape with your toes against the front edge of 1 tape. Place hands on your hips. Step forward as far as you can with 1 leg and return to the start position in 1 step. Your goal is to step just across the farthest tape as fast as possible.
